About Me

Welcome to the personal page of Nick Stull, also known as the Tinkering Nerd. Explore my background, my current interests, and my journey towards becoming an IT professional.

Background

I'm Nick, an Information Technology student at Southwestern Michigan College, where I’m working towards a degree in Information Technology. From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how technology works, and I love breaking things down to understand them and then building them back up in a better way.

My educational journey has always been intertwined with my curiosity about how things function—whether it’s programming, networking, or tinkering with gadgets like Raspberry Pi’s and 3D printers. This site is my digital garden, where I can organize my thoughts, projects, and ideas.

Current Pursuits

Currently, I’m diving deep into:

  • Programming: I’ve been working on projects using Python, PowerShell, and JavaScript, and exploring automation scripts and tools that can improve productivity.
  • Docker & Networking: I'm using Docker to containerize applications and studying networking to understand how systems communicate at both small and large scales.
  • 3D Printing: I enjoy experimenting with 3D printing to create tools and gadgets. It's a creative outlet that combines technical skills with practical problem-solving.
  • IT Helpdesk: I’m building my skills in troubleshooting, diagnosing, and solving IT problems, aiming to become proficient in customer support and IT services.
